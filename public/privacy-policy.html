Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 5, 2026. outcomestar is operated by SRJ Consulting Services. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the special protections applied to children's information.

1. What we collect

  • Account data — parent name, display name, email; optional student email.
  • Student records you enter — academics, activities, athletics, tests, milestones, documents, and media your family chooses to store.
  • Technical data — IP address for rate limiting and security logs; no advertising trackers, no third-party analytics on the app.

2. How we use it

Only to operate the service for your family: storing your records, generating the documents and websites you request, sending verification and service emails, and securing the platform. We do not sell personal information. We do not use your family's records to train AI models. We show no advertising.

3. Children's privacy (COPPA)

  • Accounts are created and controlled by a parent or legal guardian; the parent is the data controller for the student's record.
  • Signup for students under 13 requires verified parental consent; until our verification process is enabled, under-13 self-directed signup is blocked and handled through support@outcomestar.app.
  • Student public websites enforce platform rules regardless of settings: first name only on public pages, no address or phone number, nondescript site links for minors, no search-engine indexing for younger age bands, and comment moderation where comments exist.
  • Parents may review, correct, export, or delete their child's information at any time from the account or by contacting us.

4. Student emails

A student email, if provided, is used only to verify eligibility for free access and for age-appropriate account features. It is never sold, shared, or used for marketing.

5. Where data lives and how it's protected

Data is stored in the United States with per-family isolation enforced at the database layer (row-level security), encrypted transport, encrypted storage of sensitive identifiers, and time-limited signed links for private files. Access by us is limited to operating and supporting the service.

6. Sharing

We share data only: (a) with processors needed to run the service (hosting, email delivery, optional translation), bound to use it solely for us; (b) when you direct it — e.g., a public showcase page or a generated document you send; (c) when the law requires it.

7. Retention and deletion

Data is kept while your account is active. On account deletion we remove personal data within 30 days, except minimal records we must keep for legal, security, or audit reasons. Backups age out on a fixed schedule.

8. Your choices

Export everything at any time; correct anything from the portal; delete the account entirely; opt out of any non-essential email. Questions or requests: support@outcomestar.app.
